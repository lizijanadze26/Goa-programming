Tennis

about tennis:

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played in singles (one player against another) or doubles (two players against two). Players use rackets to hit a felt-covered rubber
ball over a net onto the opponent's court. The objective is to score points by making it difficult for the opponent to return the ball within the court's boundaries.

Tennis is a popular racket sport played individually or in pairs, with a rich history and a global following, featuring major tournaments like the Grand Slams.
